Welcome to ADHD Analytics

This project seeks to explore the comorbidity of mental illness in individuals with ADHD.
All elements within are built on upon the Hyperaktiv dataset.
In individuals with ADHD, the presence of a comorbid mental health condition is extremely common. In fact, 33% of children with ADHD have a comorbid condition and the prevalance of comorbid conditions is nearly 3x higher in individuals with ADHD compared to their neurotypical counterparts. Individuals with ADHD are also at a higher risk of having a learning disability and adults with ADHD have a 70% higher prevalence of obesity compared to the normal population.
